Introduction

This study focuses on soil nutrient analysis in Benguet, using satellite imagery and ground-truth soil data. It integrates field measurements, terrain attributes, and spectral indices to evaluate soil properties such as nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, and pH. The research develops machine learning models to predict soil nutrient levels and provide crop-specific fertilizer recommendations for improved agricultural management.

🗺️ Study Area & Data Sources

Location

Benguet Province, Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR), Philippines

Data Sources

  • Ground-truth soil samples: Field measurements from Benguet
  • Satellite imagery: Sentinel-2 multispectral data (10m resolution)
  • Terrain data: Digital Elevation Model (DEM)
  • Spectral indices: NDVI, EVI, SAVI, GNDVI for vegetation and soil analysis
