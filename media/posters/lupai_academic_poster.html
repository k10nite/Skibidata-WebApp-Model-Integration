LupAI: Satellite-Based Fertilizer Recommendation System for Smallholder Farmers in the Philippines

Leveraging Sentinel-2 Imagery and AI to Democratize Precision Agriculture

Research Team, Computer Science Department Cordillera Administrative Region Agricultural Research

Problem Statement

85% Smallholder Farms
(<2 hectares)
₱18.5B Annual Fertilizer
Wastage
  • Traditional soil testing costs PHP 2,000-5,000 per analysis
  • Over-fertilization wastes PHP 18.5 billion annually
  • Limited access to agricultural extension services in remote areas

Objectives

  • Provide free, accessible soil nutrient analysis
  • Reduce fertilizer costs by 30-40%
  • Increase crop yields through precision fertilization
  • Empower farmers with data-driven decisions
  • Support sustainable agricultural practices
