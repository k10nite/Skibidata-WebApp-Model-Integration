Filipino smallholder farmers face significant barriers to precision agriculture. Traditional soil testing is expensive and inaccessible, leading to inefficient fertilizer use that wastes resources and degrades soil health.

Objectives

  • Provide free, accessible soil nutrient analysis via satellite imagery
  • Reduce fertilizer costs by 30-40% through precision recommendations
  • Increase crop yields through data-driven fertilization decisions
  • Empower farmers with actionable, location-specific insights
  • Support sustainable agricultural practices across the Philippines
