Filipino smallholder farmers face significant barriers to precision agriculture. Traditional soil testing is expensive and inaccessible, leading to inefficient fertilizer use that wastes resources and degrades soil health in the CAR region.

Research Objectives

  • Provide free, accessible soil nutrient analysis via Sentinel-2 satellite imagery
  • Reduce fertilizer costs by 30-40% through precision recommendations
  • Increase crop yields through data-driven fertilization decisions
  • Empower farmers with actionable, location-specific insights
  • Support sustainable agricultural practices across the Philippines

Conclusion & Future Work
