Time Signals

SignalMC Action

5 min leftPrepare transition

Summary of BOTH Moments

  • Opening greeting: "Good morning, everyone!"
  • After panelist intro: "Thank you for being with us today."
  • Before starting: "Alright, let's begin!"
  • Each team welcome: "Please welcome [Team Name]."
  • Each team applause: "Let's give a round of applause..."
  • Break announcements: "Enjoy your lunch/break!"
  • Post-break: "Welcome back, everyone!"
  • Final applause: "Let's give one final round..."
  • Encouragement: "This is just one milestone..."
  • Sign-off: "Thank you... Good afternoon, and God bless!"
